Basic Hosting

✅ Lower cost (₹1,500–₹4,000/year)
✅ Suitable for small personal websites or portfolios
❌ Slower performance when many visitors use the site
❌ Limited resources and support

Professional Hosting

✅ Faster loading speed
✅ Better uptime and reliability
✅ More storage and bandwidth
✅ Better security for customer inquiries and contact forms
✅ Easier to grow as the business gets more clients

Recommendation

If the insurance agency is a real business that will receive customer inquiries, policy requests, or claim assistance requests through the website, choose Professional Hosting.
